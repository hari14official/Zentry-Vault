Welcome to Zentry Vault

At Zentry Vault, we believe in empowering individuals and businesses to take control of their data security. In today's digital world, protecting sensitive information isn't just a necessity—it's a right. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating innovative, easy-to-use encryption software that provides unmatched security while fitting seamlessly into your daily life.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to protect your privacy and stop data theft. Whether you're safeguarding personal files or managing business-critical information, Zentry Vault delivers robust solutions designed to keep your data secure from unauthorized access.

Our Story

Our journey in file security began back in 2025, when our lead developer created highly successful file encryption applications for Windows, later expanding to macOS and Android. The apps quickly gained widespread popularity, amassing over one million active users worldwide.

By 2026, we had achieved everything envisioned, and saw an opportunity to focus on new challenges in secure file encryption. After transferring ownership to a new company, the team set out to create something even more advanced. Drawing on years of experience and innovation, Zentry Vault was born to redefine secure file encryption for the modern age.

Founded in 2025, Zentry Vault was built with a vision to make advanced encryption accessible and easy to use. We recognised that many people found encryption tools overly complex, so we set out to create software that combines powerful, industry-standard security with a user-friendly experience. Today, Zentry Vault helps thousands of individuals and businesses secure their digital assets worldwide.

Zentry Vault works for personal use and businesses alike.
